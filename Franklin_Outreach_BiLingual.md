## ENGLISH VERSION

Hey Franklin 👋

Now that the LinkLogis deal is almost done, I've been thinking about how to make your capital sourcing smoother.

Onboarding Wilson and Chemtank was tough—lots of meetings, back-and-forth between traders and capital teams. It got me thinking: what if traders could prep pitches against investor mandates *before* the manual back-and-forth starts?

**The insight came from Garry Tan (President/CEO of Y Combinator).**

He released Gstack—a Claude Code framework that applies **forcing questions** to pressure-test ideas before engineering time is wasted. At YC office hours, ~1 in 3 projects get abandoned because they don't survive scrutiny.

I realized the same methodology works for investor mandates. So I built a working pilot this week:

**3 AI agents (Process, Legal, Compliance)** evaluate deals against investor mandates using forcing questions. Instead of vague criteria ("good fit"), every decision is transparent + repeatable. Mandates become JSON schemas. Deals get evaluated in ~3 seconds instead of 2-4 weeks.

The beauty: traders can use AI to adapt their pitches to investor requirements *before* submitting. LinkLogis keeps all sensitive info (cost of capital, internal hierarchy) completely separate.

**Why LinkLogis is perfect for this:**
- You're already doing institutional capital deployment in trade receivables
- You have real investor mandates (not theoretical)
- You can test: does this make it easier for traders to self-prep using AI?

**4-week pilot:**
1. Light MOU
2. LinkLogis submits a deal proposal through the system
3. Get evaluated by 3 agents (process ✓ legal ✓ compliance ✓)
4. Give feedback on decision quality vs. your manual experience
5. Help refine the forcing questions

This could grow into something bigger—a mandate standardization play for the entire institutional capital market. Worth a conversation?

我意识到这个methodology也适用于投资者的mandate。所以这周我就快速构建了一个working pilot：

**3个AI agents（流程、法律、合规）** 用forcing questions来评估deals是否符合投资者mandates。与其用模糊的标准（"fit不fit"），每个决策都是透明的、可重复的。Mandates变成JSON schemas。Deals的评估从2-4周降到~3秒。

最妙的是：交易员可以用AI在提交之前就根据投资者要求调整pitch。LinkLogis所有敏感信息（融资成本、内部架构）完全独立保存。

**为什么LinkLogis最适合做这个试点：**
- 你们已经在做机构投资者的应收账款融资
- 你们有真实的投资者mandates（不是理论的）
- 你们可以测试：这套系统能不能帮交易员更好地用AI自我调整？

**4周的试点计划：**
1. 签简单的MOU
2. LinkLogis通过系统提交一份deal proposal
3. 获得3个agents的评估（流程✓ 法律✓ 合规✓）
4. 反馈评估质量 vs 手工对接的体验
5. 帮我们优化forcing questions

这有可能发展成更大的东西—整个机构资本市场的mandate标准化。有兴趣讨论一下吗？
